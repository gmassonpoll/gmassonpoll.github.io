SCRIBBLES

Scribbles
They drip like acid
Infecting my bloodstream
Accumulating

Beneath the surface they fester
Scratching the fleshy tissue of humanity
Incising
Draining the wounds

Forced open memories
Past tendencies
Scribbling
Inscribed in the bones

Pulsing
Aching for the snap of a band
A stab in the back
A cut to the throat

Fading in and out
Conscious evanescing
The scribbles cover the page
Black as night
