LATE SUNNY AFTERNOONS

afternoon aching paired with a
new found addiction to burning cinnamon incense until
clouds seep their way over the mountain tops
shadows crossing, cascading slowly on these beige walls
of which I despise so dearly
but have yet to paint over in my years of residency

their mellow tone seems to have grown on me
perhaps even shown me a little sense in the fact that
not everything needs to be stark white and sterile
for that, at least, I appreciate their faded embrace
hugging me as the tendrils of smoke dance their way upwards,
my consciousness with them,
journeying beyond this self proclaimed box

to be in this warmth alone is not comforting,
but instead comfortable
which, if you think about it
makes perfect sense
and yet none at all

as do many afternoons spent drifting around these worn into sheets,
soft in their nature
brought back by the people who lay beneath them, beneath each other

my, the feeling of being underneath you is so nice I wonder sometimes
it might be better to melt into one being
to which perhaps, I think,
we may have already done so

but although you fit so nicely in tangled puzzles with me, I am at ease with myself

it is simply the fact that all I choose to be grows tenfold with you
and that my dearest is why think so fondly of you on these late,
sunny afternoons
