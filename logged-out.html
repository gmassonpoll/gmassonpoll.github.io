LOGGED OUT

Breathing through the sharp pain of the morning's embrace,
My lungs fill with a sense of wholeness,
A perfect moment

Birds unspoken;
Time’s frozen;
Lights shyly dusting the hills

Undressing every feeling, every sound, every taste
I’m introduced to a new, alluring version of the world I've previously known
This nameless version so raw, so precious,
Its flaws harmonising to create a world ingrown

Daybreak’s broken;
Sky’s awoken;
Crisp air sending my neck chills

But as quickly as this moment came,
It's lost
Slipping slowly out of that singular, seraphic breath
